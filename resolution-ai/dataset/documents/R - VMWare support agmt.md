**VMWARE SUPPORT AGREEMENT**

**DATE:** 1 April 2009

**BETWEEN:**

(1) **THE SECRETARY OF STATE FOR COMMUNITIES AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT** acting through the **DEPARTMENT FOR COMMUNITIES AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT** (the "Authority"); and

(2) **E-NIK LTD** (the "Supplier").

**1\. DEFINITIONS**

1.1 "Effective Date" means 1 April 2009\. 1.2 "Support Services" means the technical support and maintenance services for VMware software infrastructure as detailed in Schedule 1\. 1.3 "Term" means the period from the Effective Date until 30 September 2011\.

**2\. TERM AND DURATION**

2.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue in force for a fixed period of two and a half years, expiring on 30 September 2011, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement.

**3\. PROVISION OF SERVICES**

3.1 The Supplier shall provide the Support Services for the Authority's virtual server environment in accordance with the Service Level Agreement attached hereto.

**4\. CHARGES AND PAYMENT SCHEDULE**

4.1 In consideration of the provision of the Support Services, the Authority shall pay the Support Fees as set out below.

4.2 **Support Fees Structure:** The Support Fees are fixed annual charges payable in advance as follows:

* **Year 1 (1 April 2009 – 31 March 2010):** £45,000.00 (Payable on commencement)  
* **Year 2 (1 April 2010 – 31 March 2011):** £45,000.00 (Payable on 1 April 2010\)  
* **Final Period (1 April 2011 – 30 September 2011):** £22,500.00 (Payable on 1 April 2011\)

4.3 The Parties acknowledge that the fee for the Final Period represents exactly 50% of the annual Support Fee, allocated expressly in respect of the last six months of the Term.

**5\. WARRANTIES**

5.1 The Supplier warrants that it has the necessary expertise and accreditation from VMware to provide the Support Services.

**6\. LIABILITY**

6.1 The Supplier’s liability under this Agreement shall be limited to the total Support Fees paid during the preceding 12 months.

**7\. INVOICING AND VAT**

7.1 The Supplier shall invoice the Authority in accordance with the schedule in Clause 4.2. The Authority shall pay such amount with VAT (if applicable) within 30 days from the date of the invoice.

**8\. GOVERNING LAW**

8.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of England and Wales.
