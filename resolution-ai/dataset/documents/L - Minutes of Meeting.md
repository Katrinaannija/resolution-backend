**MINUTES OF MEETING**

**Subject:** General review & engagement strategy **Date:** 17 December 2010 **Location:** Department for Communities and Local Government, Eland House **Chair:** Ms. Dawett (E-Nik Ltd)

**Attendees:**

* Ms. Dawett (E-Nik Ltd)  
* Ms. Kate Kedge (Department for Communities and Local Government)  
* \[Other Departmental Representatives\]

**1\. Review of Current Projects** The meeting was convened to review the current status of ongoing consultancy assignments and the overall engagement strategy.

Discussion was held regarding the Government's announcement regarding the future closure of the Government Offices for the Regions (GOR). Ms. Dawett noted that E-Nik’s understanding is that current projects involve a broader scope beyond the GOR and should remain unaffected by the announcement.

**2\. Engagement Strategy and Future Provisions** The Department clarified its position regarding the continuation of the supplier’s engagement during the transition period.

**Action Item:** Ms. Kate Kedge **requested that enik continue providing services until advised otherwise.**

**3\. Next Steps** E-Nik Ltd to maintain current resource availability and continue delivery of support for GO ICT Systems.
