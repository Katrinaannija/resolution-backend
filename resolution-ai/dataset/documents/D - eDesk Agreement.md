**eDESK SOFTWARE MAINTENANCE AND SUPPORT AGREEMENT**

DATE: 1 April 2009

BETWEEN:

(1) THE SECRETARY OF STATE FOR COMMUNITIES AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT acting through the DEPARTMENT FOR COMMUNITIES AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT (the "Customer");

and

(2) E-NIK LTD (the "Supplier").

1\. DEFINITIONS  
1.1 "Agreement" means this software maintenance and support agreement.  
1.2 "eDesk Software" means the proprietary software solution provided by the Supplier.  
1.3 "Term" means the period from 1 April 2009 to 30 September 2011\.

2\. DURATION  
2.1 This Agreement shall commence on 1 April 2009 and shall remain in full force and effect until 30 September 2011\.

3\. SCOPE OF SERVICES  
3.1 The Supplier shall provide maintenance, updates, and technical support for the eDesk Software as described in Schedule A.

4\. MAINTENANCE FEES / COSTS  
4.1 The Customer agrees to pay the Maintenance Fees for the support of the eDesk Software.  
4.2 Payment Schedule: The Fees are payable annually in advance for the first two years, with a final payment for the remaining six-month period as follows:  
    (a) Year 1 (1 April 2009 – 31 March 2010): £20,000.00  
    (b) Year 2 (1 April 2010 – 31 March 2011): £20,000.00  
    (c) Final Period (1 April 2011 – 30 September 2011): £9,800.00  
4.3 The Parties acknowledge that the fee for the Final Period is a fixed sum allocated for the remaining term, representing a figure slightly less than half of the annual Maintenance Fee.

5\. WARRANTIES  
5.1 The Supplier warrants that the eDesk Software will perform substantially in accordance with its documentation.

6\. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY  
6.1 The Supplier retains all title, copyright, and other proprietary rights in the eDesk Software.

7\. CONFIDENTIALITY  
7.1 Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the other party's proprietary information.

8\. LIABILITY  
8.1 The Supplier’s total liability under this Agreement is limited to the amount of Fees paid by the Customer in the preceding contract year.

9\. INVOICING AND PAYMENT TERMS  
9.1 The Supplier shall invoice the Customer in respect of the Fees and the Customer shall pay such amount with VAT (if applicable) within 30 days from the date of the invoice.

10\. GENERAL  
10.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.
