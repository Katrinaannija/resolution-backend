**INTERNAL MEMORANDUM**

**TO:** Mr. Culver, Head of Procurement **FROM:** Mr. Bentley, Project Representative **DATE:** 15 January 2009 **SUBJECT:** Historical Usage and Renewal of Consultancy Services – E-Nik Ltd / Fujitsu Framework

**1\. Purpose** The purpose of this memo is to document the historical relationship between the Department and E-Nik Ltd in preparation for the upcoming contract renewal scheduled for 1 April 2009\. This agreement is intended to sit as Appendix 1 to the agreement between the Department and Fujitsu Services Ltd.

**2\. Historic Performance and Usage** E-Nik Ltd has gained and retained the confidence of the Department through the provision of consultancy services on a number of occasions previously.

A review of our usage indicates that, by 2008, the Department was purchasing approximately **800 days of consultancy work in each year** from this supplier. This volume reflects the ongoing requirements of our IT projects.

**3\. Rate Analysis** The historical pricing structure for E-Nik Ltd has been as follows:

* **Daily Rates:** Between **£750 and £1,200 per day**.  
* **Variance:** Rates have varied strictly according to the seniority of the consultant provided and the work done.  
* **Value for Money:** The rates charged by E-Nik Ltd are currently **at or slightly below the industry norm**.

**4\. Recommendation for New Agreement** To secure E-Nik's engagement for work and ensure resource availability, it is recommended that the new term includes a commitment to purchase a **minimum of 500 days** of consultancy from the Supplier per year.
