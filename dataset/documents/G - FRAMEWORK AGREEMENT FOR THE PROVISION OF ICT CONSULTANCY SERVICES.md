### **FRAMEWORK AGREEMENT FOR THE PROVISION OF ICT CONSULTANCY SERVICES**

**FRAMEWORK AGREEMENT FOR THE PROVISION OF ICT CONSULTANCY SERVICES (APPENDIX 1 TO THE MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT)**

**THIS AGREEMENT** is made on this 1st day of April 2009

**BETWEEN:**

(1) **THE SECRETARY OF STATE FOR COMMUNITIES AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT** acting through the **DEPARTMENT FOR COMMUNITIES AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT**, having its principal offices at Eland House, Bressenden Place, London, SW1E 5DU (hereinafter referred to as the "Authority" or the "Department");

**AND**

(2) **E-NIK LTD**, a company incorporated and registered in England and Wales with company number \[REDACTED\], whose registered office is at \[Address\] (hereinafter referred to as the "Supplier").

(Each a "Party" and together the "Parties")

**BACKGROUND AND RECITALS**

(A) The Authority is a ministerial department of the United Kingdom Government responsible for community cohesion, local government, and housing, and requires specialized information technology support for its Government Office (GO) ICT Systems.

(B) The Authority has previously entered into a Master Services Agreement with Fujitsu Services Ltd ("Fujitsu") for the provision of wider IT infrastructure services.

(C) The Supplier is an established provider of information technology consultancy services and has represented to the Authority that it has the requisite skill, knowledge, and personnel to perform the required services.

(D) **Purpose or Meaning:** The Parties agree that the purpose or meaning of this Agreement is to purchase Consultancy to provide Project Work, including Project Management, Scoping/Analysis and Systems Development for GO ICT Systems.

(E) The Parties intend for this Agreement (Agreement Reference: ENIK/DCLG/09/001) to operate as Appendix 1 to the existing agreement between the Authority and Fujitsu Services Ltd, acknowledging that Fujitsu may act as a paying agent for certain transactional elements as detailed in Appendix 2 attached hereto.

**NOW THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants, undertakings, and promises contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties agree as follows:

### **1\. DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION**

**1.1 Definitions** In this Agreement, the following expressions shall have the following meanings unless the context otherwise requires:

* "Agreement" means this contract, including all Schedules, Appendices, and Annexes attached hereto.  
* "Assignment Note" shall mean a mutually agreed document detailing the precise purpose of work to be carried out by the Supplier.  
* "Confidential Information" means all information, whether written or oral, which is designated as confidential or which ought reasonably to be considered confidential.  
* "Consultancy" or "Services" means the professional ICT advice, project management, and development work to be provided by the Supplier.  
* "Effective Date" means 1 April 2009\.  
* "Force Majeure" means any event outside the reasonable control of either Party affecting its ability to perform any of its obligations (other than payment) under this Agreement.  
* "GO ICT Systems" means the Government Office Information and Communication Technology infrastructure.  
* "Intellectual Property Rights" means patents, trademarks, service marks, design rights, copyright, database rights, and any other similar rights.  
* "Working Day" means Monday to Friday, excluding Bank Holidays in England and Wales.

(c) Reference to any statute or statutory provision includes a reference to that statute or statutory provision as from time to time amended, extended, or re-enacted.

### **2\. AUTHORITY’S OBLIGATIONS**

**2.1 Minimum Purchase Commitment** The Authority hereby undertakes to purchase minimum of 500 days of Consultancy from the Supplier per year based on project requirement, additional days will be required once the purchased days have been exhausted.

**2.2 Requisition Process** The Authority shall issue an Assignment Note to requisition Services from the Supplier.

**2.3 Fees and Rates** The Authority shall pay the Supplier fees at the rate of not less than £850 per day but subject to mutually agreed assignment notes for each change request. This rate may be revised upward at the time of issue of an Assignment Note to reflect the complexity of the Services requisitioned or the level of skill required for the provision of the Services.

**2.4 Payment Terms** Payment of the fees shall be made within 30 days of the date of a valid invoice from the Supplier.

**2.5 General Cooperation** The Authority shall provide the Supplier with reasonable access to the Authority's premises, data, and personnel as may be reasonably required for the Supplier to perform the Services.

### **3\. SUPPLIER’S OBLIGATIONS**

**3.1 Provision of Services** The Supplier shall provide the Services with reasonable skill, care, and diligence in accordance with the best industry practice and the terms of this Agreement. The Supplier warrants that its personnel are suitably qualified and experienced to perform the tasks assigned to them.

**3.3 Compliance with Policies** The Supplier shall comply with all reasonable health and safety, security, and environmental policies of the Authority while on the Authority's premises.

**3.4 Independent Contractor** The Supplier is an independent contractor, and nothing in this Agreement shall render the Supplier an employee, worker, agent, or partner of the Authority.

**3.5 Conflict of Interest** The Supplier shall take all reasonable steps to ensure that no conflict of interest arises between the interests of the Authority and any other client of the Supplier.

**3.6 Sub-contracting** The Supplier shall not sub-contract any of its obligations under this Agreement without the prior written consent of the Authority.

**3.8 Reporting** Supplier is obliged to provide concise accounts of resources used on a monthly basis as an excel document which shows work priorities, days used, days remaining and days purchased.

### **4\. WARRANTIES AND INDEMNITIES**

**4.1** The Supplier warrants that it has full power and authority to enter into and perform this Agreement and that the person signing this Agreement on behalf of the Supplier has been duly authorized to do so.

**4.2** The Supplier shall indemnify and keep indemnified the Authority against all actions, proceedings, costs, claims, demands, liabilities, losses, and expenses whatsoever, whether arising in tort (including negligence) or contract, arising out of or in connection with any breach of this Agreement by the Supplier.

### **5\. CONFIDENTIALITY AND DATA PROTECTION**

**5.1** Each Party shall keep confidential all Confidential Information received from the other Party and shall use such information only for the purposes of this Agreement.

**5.2** The Supplier acknowledges that the Authority is subject to the requirements of the Freedom of Information Act 2000 and shall assist and cooperate with the Authority to enable the Authority to comply with its information disclosure obligations.

### **6\. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY**

**6.1** All Intellectual Property Rights in any materials, reports, software, or data created or developed by the Supplier specifically for the Authority under this Agreement shall vest in the Authority upon creation.

### **7\. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY**

**7.1** Nothing in this Agreement shall limit or exclude either Party's liability for death or personal injury caused by its negligence, or for fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation.

**7.2** Subject to Clause 7.1, the Authority’s total aggregate liability under or in connection with this Agreement shall be limited to the total charges paid or payable during the Term.

### **8\. TERM AND TERMINATION**

**8.1 Duration and Notice** This Agreement shall commence on 1 April 2009 and continue until 30 September 2011\. This Agreement is terminable by 12 months written notice with 12 months cancellation from the date of notice received by either party.

**8.2 Termination for Cause** Either Party may terminate this Agreement with immediate effect by notice in writing to the other Party if the other Party commits a material breach of this Agreement which is not capable of remedy, or if capable of remedy, is not remedied within 30 days of receipt of notice of the breach.

**8.3 Consequences of Termination** Upon termination or expiry of this Agreement, the Supplier shall immediately return to the Authority all property, data, and Confidential Information belonging to the Authority.
